Java Developer | Software & Web Development | Game Modder
---------------------------------------------------------

I am a Java-focused developer with experience in software development, web design, and game modding. I specialize in building structured, maintainable systems with a strong emphasis on clarity, scalability, and long-term usability.

My work includes developing custom Minecraft mods, designing web interfaces, and creating software solutions that are both functional and adaptable. I follow a disciplined approach to coding, ensuring that all projects are well-organized and easy to extend or modify.

I continuously refine my skills by exploring new technologies, improving development workflows, and building projects that challenge both technical and creative problem-solving.

* 🌍  I'm based in India
* 🖥️  See my portfolio at [Kingodogo.com](http://Kingodogo.com)
* ✉️  You can contact me at [kingodogo@outlook.com](mailto:kingodogo@outlook.com)
* 🚀  I'm currently working on [Buildscape - a Minecraft project focused on Building blocks and Functional features to those building blocks involving many custom aspect of 3d item rendering.](http://Buildscape.online)
* 🧠  I'm currently learning Multi-language development across Java, Python, Rust, C#, and JavaScript, with a focus on backend architecture, AI infrastructure, and scalable web systems. Expanding into TypeScript, SQL, and systems-level programming for high-performance applications.
* 👥  I'm looking to collaborate on Game systems, Minecraft modding projects, and scalable software development initiatives.
* 💬  Ask me about I approach development with a structured and systems-oriented mindset. Before implementing any feature, I plan out the architecture and logic flow, often breaking problems down into smaller, manageable components. I prefer visualizing systems beforehand—mapping out how different parts interact—so implementation is guided by a clear direction rather than trial and error. My workflow focuses on building maintainable and scalable solutions. I write code with long-term evolution in mind, ensuring that systems remain adaptable, easy to modify, and well-organized. Clear structure, logical separation of components, and consistent patterns are priorities in all my projects. I tend to work in extended, focused sessions, most actively between 6:00 AM and 12:00 AM (GMT+5:30). This allows me to stay deeply engaged with complex problems and maintain continuity across development cycles. I also pay close attention to detail and refinement. I iterate on systems multiple times, improving both functionality and structure until they meet a high standard of quality. My goal is not just to make things work, but to ensure they are robust, efficient, and built to last.
